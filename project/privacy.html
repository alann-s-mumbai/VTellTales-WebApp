Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 11, 2025

At VTellTales, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our educational storytelling platform. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the platform, such as your name, email address, and school information.

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate the platform. This information may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and pages viewed.

Content Data

We collect the stories, images, and multimedia content that you create and upload to the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive to:

  • Facilitate account creation and authentication
  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Send administrative information and updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends

3. Children's Privacy

VTellTales is designed for use by educators. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The platform is intended to be used by teachers to create content for children, not for direct use by children themselves. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

4. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

5. Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following situations:

1

With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf

2

For Legal Purposes: We may disclose your information where required by law or to protect our rights

3

With Your Consent: We may share your information with your explicit consent

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access

Request access to your personal data

Correction

Request correction of inaccurate data

Deletion

Request deletion of your data

Portability

Request transfer of your data

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our platform and store certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
